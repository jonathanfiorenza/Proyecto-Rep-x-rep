Recipes

cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup raisins (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C)
  2. Mix oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl
  3. In another bowl, mix melted butter, eggs, and raisins (if using)
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until well combined.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of mixture onto a baking sheet lined with parchment pape
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until cookies are golden brown

Grilled salmon with asparagus

Salmon is rich in omega-3s, and asparagus is an excellent source of fiber.

Ingredients.

  • 1.3 kg of salmon fillets
  • 32 green asparagus
  • 32 fresh purple asparagus
  • 400g margarine
  • 3 lemons
  • 40g fresh dill
  • salt
  • ground black pepper

Instructions.

  1. We start by cleaning the salmon and cutting the base of the asparagus.
  2. On a non-stick grill plate, we break up a nut of margarine.
  3. We place the salmon skin-side down and let it brown without moving it.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, turn it over, and cook for 4 more minutes.
  5. Then we brown the asparagus, turning them occasionally so that they brown evenly.
  6. Finally, we plate the asparagus at the bottom of the plate, the salmon on top, and the asparagus tips, chopped dill, and lemon zest as the final topping.

chicken sandwich

The perfect recipe for a light chicken sandwich with cream cheese.

Ingredients.

  • Seeded bread
  • Light cream cheese
  • Arugula
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • avocado

Instructions.

  1. Cut the chicken breast into thin strips.
  2. In a pan, grill the breasts until well cooked.
  3. Meanwhile, cut the bread into slices and spread a layer of spreadable cheese
  4. Add the lettuce, avocado and salt and pepper.
  5. ¡ready to enjoy!.

meditation

Guided meditation can be very effective for calming anxiety and stress. The practice involves following the instructions of an audio or guide, which helps you focus on your breathing, relax your body and mind, and visualize situations or sensations that promote calm.

Basic steps of a guided meditation for anxiety:

  1. Find a quiet place: Find a space where you can sit or lie down without interruptions.
  2. Get into a comfortable position: Sit with your back straight, or lie on your back with your arms relaxed at your sides.
  3. Close your eyes: This will help you focus on the internal experience.
  4. Listen to the guide: Pay attention to the voice prompts that will guide you through the meditation.
  5. Focus on your breathing: Breathing is the main anchor in meditation. Observe how the air enters and leaves your body, without forcing the rhythm.
  6. Relax your body: Follow the instructions to relax each part of your body, from your feet to your head.
  7. Visualiza imágenes relajantes: La guía puede pedirte que imagines lugares tranquilos, como un jardín o una playa, o que visualices sensaciones de calma.
  8. Accept your thoughts: Thoughts may appear, but don't cling to them. Simply acknowledge them and let them pass, like clouds in the sky.
  9. End the meditation: When the guide tells you to, slowly return to awareness of your surroundings, gently moving your hands and feet.
