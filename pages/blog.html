Recipes

cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup raisins (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C)
  2. Mix oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl
  3. In another bowl, mix melted butter, eggs, and raisins (if using)
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until well combined.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of mixture onto a baking sheet lined with parchment pape
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until cookies are golden brown

Grilled salmon with asparagus

Salmon is rich in omega-3s, and asparagus is an excellent source of fiber.

Ingredients.

  • 1.3 kg of salmon fillets
  • 32 green asparagus
  • 32 fresh purple asparagus
  • 400g margarine
  • 3 lemons
  • 40g fresh dill
  • salt
  • ground black pepper

Instructions.

  1. We start by cleaning the salmon and cutting the base of the asparagus.
  2. On a non-stick grill plate, we break up a nut of margarine.
  3. We place the salmon skin-side down and let it brown without moving it.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, turn it over, and cook for 4 more minutes.
  5. Then we brown the asparagus, turning them occasionally so that they brown evenly.
  6. Finally, we plate the asparagus at the bottom of the plate, the salmon on top, and the asparagus tips, chopped dill, and lemon zest as the final topping.

chicken sandwich

The perfect recipe for a light chicken sandwich with cream cheese.

Ingredients.

  • Seeded bread
  • Light cream cheese
  • Arugula
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • avocado

Instructions.

  1. Cut the chicken breast into thin strips.
  2. In a pan, grill the breasts until well cooked.
  3. Meanwhile, cut the bread into slices and spread a layer of spreadable cheese
  4. Add the lettuce, avocado and salt and pepper.
  5. ¡ready to enjoy!.
